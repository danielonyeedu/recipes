Beef suya

Description

Suya is a traditionally smoked spice meat skewer which orginates in the Hausa Land, the Northern region of Nigeria. It is generally made with skewered beef, ram, or chicken.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable or Peanut Oil
  • 1/4 cup Ginger Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Cayenne Powder
  • 1/4 cup Paprika Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Onion Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon powder
  • Suya Spice (Yaji)

Steps

  1. Gather your spices (except for yaji) and mix well.
  2. Slice meat into small pieces.
  3. Mix spices with meat and oil until well combined.
  4. Skewer meat and cover the area with the yaji spice.
  5. Put skewered meat in a 375Â° heated oven for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Once time is up remove the meat from the oven and enjoy your meal!
